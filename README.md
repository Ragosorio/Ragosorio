## About me

I'm a Full Stack Developer from Guatemala City, currently studying **Systems Engineering** at Universidad de San Carlos de Guatemala, with a Full Stack JavaScript path completed at Platzi.

I work across the whole stack — from architecting multi-tenant backends and event-driven workflow engines, to shipping polished frontends that people actually enjoy using. Most of my work lives where those two meet: systems that have to be **correct under load** and **pleasant to use** at the same time.

Right now I'm at **ROO Guatemala**, where I architected a 26-module multi-tenant backend and led the frontend migration to a shared web + mobile monorepo. Before that I spent two years building booking platforms that handled **700+ family reservations**, and internal systems for ~30 agency clients across Guatemala and the US.

- 🎓 Systems Engineering @ USAC *(in progress)*
- 💼 Full Stack Developer @ ROO Guatemala
